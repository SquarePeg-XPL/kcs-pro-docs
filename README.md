## About KCS Pro

KCS Pro helps teams maintain healthy, actionable knowledge bases by:
- Analyzing content for staleness, duplicates, and readability
- Providing AI-powered improvement suggestions
- Tracking health score improvements over time
- Prioritizing pages that need attention
